Where things stand today

The current site has a few things working against it.

That's not a criticism — it's just that the web has moved on, and the site hasn't kept up. Here's what residents and visitors are running into:

📱
Doesn't work on phones
The site was built for desktop screens. On a phone, text is tiny, buttons are hard to tap, and navigation breaks. Most people browse on mobile today.
📅
Outdated content everywhere
Event dates from months ago, committee agendas marked "not yet posted," and links that go nowhere. Stale content erodes trust quickly.
🗺️
Hard to find things
The navigation has 5 top-level sections with deep submenus. Finding something simple — like a contact number or a form — takes too many clicks.
🔗
Broken & confusing links
Several links point to a separate domain (skylinehouseowners.com) without explanation. Others lead to PDFs that could live as proper web pages.
🎨
Visual design is dated
Table-based layout, no consistent visual style, and a web hit counter at the bottom. It doesn't reflect the quality of the community it represents.
🔒
No clear residents' section
There's no obvious, easy-to-find hub for residents — forms, announcements, emergency contacts. Everything is scattered across dozens of pages.
Today
  • Navigation with 30+ links buried in submenus
  • No mobile-friendly layout
  • Content updated irregularly, dates often wrong
  • Important info (emergency contacts, forms) hard to find
  • Two separate domains causing confusion
After redesign
  • Clear, simple navigation — find anything in 2 clicks
  • Fully responsive, works great on every device
  • Easy content management — anyone can update it
  • Resident hub with forms, contacts, and announcements front and center
  • One unified domain, consistent experience throughout

What I'm proposing

A clean, modern site that residents actually want to use.

Nothing overcomplicated. Just a well-built website that's easy to navigate, easy to keep up-to-date, and genuinely useful for the people who live at Skyline House.

01
Mobile-first redesign
Built to look great on phones, tablets, and desktops from the ground up. No pinching, no zooming, no frustration — just a site that works wherever residents are.
02
Simplified navigation
A clear structure with the most-used sections front and center: Announcements, Resident Resources, Board & Committees, and Contact. Everything else neatly organized underneath.
03
Resident hub
One dedicated section for residents with forms, emergency contacts, event calendar, and announcements — all in one place, easy to update by management.
04
Easy content management
Built so non-technical staff can update announcements, post agendas, and change event dates without touching any code. No more outdated content.
05
Visual refresh
A professional, clean look that reflects the quality of Skyline House — consistent fonts, colors, and layout throughout every page.
06
Migration & training included
All existing content migrated to the new site. Staff training included so your team feels confident managing it independently going forward.
